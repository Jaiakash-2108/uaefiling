Your Questions

Frequently asked.
Honestly answered.

Can I get a licence if I'm on a spouse or parent visa?
Yes. You can hold your own freelance licence while on a dependent visa. Your sponsor doesn't need to issue an NOC. One of the biggest myths we clear up in our first call.
Do I need a physical office?
No. You use a flexi-desk — a shared workspace address. Satisfies the legal requirement. No expensive lease. Included in most packages.
Can I sponsor my family on a freelance visa?
Yes, in most cases. You can sponsor spouse and children. Eligibility depends on free zone and income. We confirm this in your free consultation.
Will I actually open a bank account?
Yes. Not all UAE banks accept freelancers, but several genuinely do. We know which ones and guide you. This is included in all packages.
How long does everything take?
Licence: 3–5 working days. With visa: 2–4 weeks total. We update you at every step. No surprises. No waiting in the dark.
